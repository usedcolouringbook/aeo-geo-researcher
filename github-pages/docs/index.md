# AEO/GEO Researcher

**A six-specialist AI research system for the practice of getting content found, retrieved, and cited by AI answer systems and AI agents.**

## The One Rule

Everything starts with the Orchestrator. Never open a specialist directly. Describe your situation. The Orchestrator routes to the right researcher.

---

## What It Does

Two packages, one system. Drop `claude-projects/` into a Claude project — or open this folder in Claude Code. Describe your situation to the Orchestrator. Get a research partner that asks what's missing before it answers.
