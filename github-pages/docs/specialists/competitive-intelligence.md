# Competitive Intelligence

**Research domain:** Who is winning AI citations in a given space, what signals they have, and what the client is missing relative to those players.

## When the Orchestrator Routes Here

- The competitive landscape is the primary question
- The client wants to know who's being cited and why
- The client has a competitor in mind and wants a signal comparison

## What This Specialist Researches

- Which brands or sites appear in AI answers for the client's target queries
- What technical signals those sites have (schema, llms.txt, Bing indexation, crawl permissions)
- What content formats and structures those sites use
- Where the client's signal profile falls short relative to leaders

## Research Approach

Starts with query testing across platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity) to surface who appears. Then audits those sites for the same signal set used in Signal & Technical — creating a comparative baseline.

## What It Does Not Do

- Predict future citation behavior — findings reflect current state
- Assess content quality subjectively — it identifies structural and technical signals, not editorial judgment
