# Content Gap

**Research domain:** What content is missing in the client's space that AI agents cannot currently answer — and what format, structure, and density that content needs.

## When the Orchestrator Routes Here

- Content strategy is the primary question
- Technical signals have already been confirmed (usually after Signal & Technical)
- The client wants to know what to build, not why their current content isn't appearing

## Prerequisite

Content gap analysis is most valuable after technical foundations are confirmed. If crawlers are blocked or Bing indexation is zero, content strategy work has no surface to land on. The Orchestrator flags this dependency at intake.

## What This Specialist Researches

- Query patterns that return no good answers across AI platforms
- Topics the client's space owns but hasn't structured for AI retrieval
- Content formats that AI systems retrieve reliably vs. formats they skip
- Factual density gaps — topics present on the site but too thin to cite

## Output Format

Gaps organized by category (topic, format, density) with a priority ranking based on query volume signal and competitive saturation.
